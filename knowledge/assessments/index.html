ASSESSMENTS™

Structured self service evaluation for defined organizational questions.

NOT EVERY QUESTION REQUIRES A FULL DIAGNOSTIC ENGAGEMENT.

WHAT THEY ARE

KINGLIS Assessments™ provide defined questions, evaluation criteria and structured outputs around specific organizational areas. Some organizational conditions can first be examined through structured evaluation.

HOW THEY WORK
  • COMPLETEWork through the defined assessment.
  • EVALUATEResponses are considered against the assessment structure.
  • RECEIVEObtain the defined findings or report associated with the assessment.
THE DISTINCTION

Assessments measure against a defined structure. Diagnostics investigate evidence to establish what is producing an outcome. They are not the same product.

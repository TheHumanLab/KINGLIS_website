Commercial disputes rarely arrive in sequence.

They arrive fragmented across emails, WhatsApp messages, correspondence, agreements, reports and supporting documents.

Before legal judgement begins, someone must reconstruct the evidential record.

KINGLIS provides Evidence Reconstruction™ — a confidential, fixed-fee service that reconstructs fragmented matter evidence into a structured, source-traceable record prepared for professional review.
01 / Definition
Evidence Reconstruction™ is the structured process of transforming fragmented matter evidence into a coherent, source-traceable evidential record before substantive legal analysis or professional judgement is applied.
It does not provide legal advice.
It does not determine liability.
It prepares the evidential record for professional review.

08 / FAQ
How is confidentiality managed?+
Every matter is handled under strict confidentiality protocols. Evidence is processed in a controlled, locally operated environment and returned directly to your legal team. We operate under NDA as standard and do not retain matter data beyond delivery unless instructed.
How is pricing determined?+
Fixed-fee per matter, determined after the Confidential Matter Assessment based on volume, fragmentation and complexity of the evidential record. No hourly billing. No variation without instruction.
What evidence can be submitted?+
Emails, WhatsApp exports, correspondence, agreements, reports, meeting minutes, PDFs, Word documents, spreadsheets and supporting documents in native format. We structure regardless of format.
How long does reconstruction take?+
Typical commercial matter: 5–10 working days from assessment to Professional Review Package, depending on volume. Urgent timelines accommodated where possible.
How are deliverables returned?+
As a structured Professional Review Package — Evidence Index™, Evidence Chronology™, and supporting registers — in both navigable and print-ready formats, with full source-traceability.
Do you use AI?+
KINGLIS uses a controlled evidence-processing workflow supported by a locally operated intelligence engine. Every matter is professionally reviewed before delivery.

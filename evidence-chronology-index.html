THE RECORD AS RECEIVED

BUILT FOR THE FILE THAT HAS BECOME DIFFICULT TO HOLD IN YOUR HEAD

Evidence Chronology™ is designed for matters where the factual record has become structurally difficult to reconstruct.

  • Multiple source types.
  • Multiple people or parties.
  • Extended sequences of events.
  • Differing accounts.
  • Decisions and actions recorded across separate sources.
  • Evidence relationships that are difficult to see document by document.
  • Material gaps in the submitted record.

Not every matter requires Evidence Chronology™.

The matters that do are usually obvious.

The file is open. The evidence is there. The record is still unclear.

THE KINGLIS MODEL

Evidence. Intelligence. Advisory.

We do not begin with a preferred solution. We begin with what the available evidence supports.

01 — EVIDENCE

Establish What Actually Happened

When the record is fragmented, disputed, or incomplete, you can't afford to guess.

We reconstruct fragmented evidence into a single, structured and source-traceable record — built to withstand scrutiny in investigation, governance, litigation and executive decision-making.

Capabilities
  • Evidence Reconstruction™ — Reconstruct available records into a structured, source-traceable account of events — built to withstand legal, regulatory and governance scrutiny, including active litigation and regulatory enforcement.
  • Document Intelligence Review — Examine your documentary record to surface inconsistencies, gaps, risks and evidentiary weaknesses before they become liabilities.
  • Workplace & Conduct Investigations — Conduct independent, impartial investigations into misconduct, governance concerns and organisational disputes — producing findings that are defensible, documented and decision-ready.
  • Litigation & Regulatory Support — Prepare and structure your evidence for legal proceedings, regulatory responses and external review — reducing exposure and accelerating resolution.
You'll Receive
  • Evidence Register

  • Executive Investigation Report
  • Document Gap Analysis

Understand What It Actually Means

Evidence alone doesn't make decisions. Intelligence does.

We transform evidence, organisational information and operational data into decision-ready intelligence — revealing the patterns, structural conditions, risks and opportunities that ordinary reviews rarely uncover.

Capabilities
  • Decision Intelligence™ — Transform complex organisational information into clear, prioritised intelligence — so leadership knows exactly what to act on.
  • Business X-Ray™ — Reveal how the organisation actually functions — not how it believes it functions.

03 — ADVISORY

Design What Comes Next

Insight without execution is expensive.

Where intelligence reveals structural risk or opportunity, we design the systems, knowledge architecture and operating models required to create lasting organisational capability — built for your organisation, not borrowed from a template.

Capabilities
  • Knowledge Architecture™ — Design how organisational knowledge is captured, structured and governed — reducing dependency on individuals and building capability that scales.
  • Founder & Leadership Advisory™ — Work directly with founders and senior leaders to structure expertise, decision-making frameworks and institutional knowledge — so the organisation can operate and grow beyond any single individual.
  • Operating Model Design — Design the governance structures, decision rights and operational frameworks required for the organisation to function with clarity, accountability and resilience.
  • AI Knowledge Readiness — Prepare organisational knowledge for responsible AI adoption — because AI is only as good as the information it is built on.

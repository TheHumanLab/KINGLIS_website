Secure Drop™

Private evidence intake for Organizational Intelligence.
Receive selected working evidence through a defined
KINGLIS submission route.

Your organization already generated the evidence. Secure Drop gives it a controlled route in.

KINGLIS Secure Drop™ is a private evidence intake application designed to receive the working material used in KINGLIS intelligence processes. Documents. Messages. Notes. Reports. Images. Working files. Select the evidence you want examined. Drop the files or folders. Submit.

WHEN IT IS RELEVANT

Organizational evidence rarely exists in one place. It sits across documents, exported messages, meeting notes, reports, working files and supporting records. Collecting that material through scattered email attachments creates unnecessary fragmentation before the intelligence work has even begun.
